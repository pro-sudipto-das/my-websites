Information We Collect

We collect information when you: Visit our website or use our online services Apply for admission to our university Register for courses or programs Use campus services as a student, faculty, or staff member Attend university events Interact with our alumni network Make donations or support the university

Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information: Contact information (name, address, email, phone) Demographic information (date of birth, gender, nationality) Educational background Financial information (for tuition, scholarships, donations) Employment information Student records and academic performance Health information (for health services, accommodations) Photos and videos (from campus events, security cameras)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our website or online services, we may automatically collect: IP address and device information Browser type and settings Referring website Pages viewed and interactions Cookies and similar technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: Process applications and enrollments Provide educational services and support Manage student records and academic programs Communicate important information Process financial transactions and aid Support campus safety and security Conduct institutional research and reporting Engage with alumni and supporters Improve our website and services Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We may share your information with: University departments and affiliated organizations Service providers who perform functions on our behalf Government agencies (for reporting, compliance) Accreditation bodies Other educational institutions (with your consent) Public directories (limited information) Emergency contacts and law enforcement (when necessary)

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: Access and review your personal information Request corrections to inaccurate information Request deletion of certain information Opt out of certain uses and disclosures Withdraw consent where applicable

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy on our website with the effective date.

About Us

National Institute of Technology (NIT) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in engineering, technology, and research. We offer cutting-edge academic programs designed to equip students with the skills needed for the global workforce. With a focus on innovation and hands-on learning, NIT fosters a dynamic and inclusive campus culture. Our highly qualified faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure a world-class education. NIT is committed to shaping the leaders of tomorrow through academic rigor and industry partnerships.

About National Institute of Technology

The National Institute of Technology (NIT) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in engineering, technology, and sciences. With a legacy of nurturing future leaders, innovators, and researchers, NIT stands as a beacon of quality education and research.

Established: 1961
Location: Hamburg, Germany.
Accreditation: AICTE, UGC, NAAC 'A' Grade
Rankings: Top 10 Engineering Institutes in Germany
